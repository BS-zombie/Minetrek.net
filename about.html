About Minetrek

Minetrek is a collaborative Minecraft server dedicated to the art of shipbuilding within the iconic universes of Star Trek and Star Wars. Here, a passionate community of players comes together to craft intricate and awe-inspiring starships that pay homage to the beloved vessels from these legendary science fiction franchises. Our server provides a creative and engaging environment where fans and builders can work together to meticulously recreate the iconic ships they admire, whether it's the USS Enterprise, Millennium Falcon, or any other famous spacecraft. Join Minetrek and be part of this thrilling journey through the stars, where imagination knows no bounds, and the adventure is limited only by your creativity.
